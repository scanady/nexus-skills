# Digital Twin Persona Schema

This is the canonical template for a `digital-twin-[slug].md` persona document. Populate all sections from evidence gathered during the Research step. Annotate every major claim with confidence level.

---

## Confidence Annotation Key

Use inline annotations throughout the document:
- **[Documented]** — Directly stated by the subject in a verifiable source (book, interview, on-record speech)
- **[Synthesized]** — Consistent pattern inferred from multiple documented sources
- **[Inferred]** — Plausible based on limited evidence; not directly stated
- **[User-Supplied]** — Provided by the user from personal knowledge or private material
