## Onboarding Emails

### New Users Series
**Trigger**: User signs up (free or trial)
**Goal**: Activate, drive to aha moment
**Length**: 5–7 emails over 14 days

| # | Timing | Purpose |
|---|--------|---------|
| 1 | Immediate | Welcome + single next step |
| 2 | Day 1 | Quick win / getting started |
| 3 | Day 3 | Key feature highlight |
| 4 | Day 5 | Success story / social proof |
| 5 | Day 7 | Check-in + offer help |
| 6 | Day 10 | Advanced tip |
| 7 | Day 14 | Upgrade prompt or next milestone |

**Key metrics**: Activation rate, feature adoption

---

### New Customers Series
**Trigger**: User converts to paid
**Goal**: Reinforce purchase decision, drive adoption, reduce early churn
**Length**: 3–5 emails over 14 days

| # | Timing | Purpose |
|---|--------|---------|
| 1 | Immediate | Thank you + what's next |
| 2 | Day 2 | Getting full value — setup checklist |
| 3 | Day 5 | Pro tips for paid features |
| 4 | Day 7 | Success story from similar customer |
| 5 | Day 14 | Check-in + introduce support resources |

**Key point**: Different from new users series — they've committed. Focus on reinforcement and expansion, not conversion.

---

### Key Onboarding Step Reminder
**Trigger**: User hasn't completed critical setup step after X time
**Goal**: Nudge completion of high-value action
**Format**: Single email or 2–3 email mini-sequence

**Example triggers**:
- Hasn't connected integration after 48 hours
- Hasn't invited team member after 3 days
- Hasn't completed profile after 24 hours

**Copy approach**: Remind what they started → explain why step matters → direct link to complete → offer help if stuck

---

### New User Invite
**Trigger**: Existing user invites teammate
**Goal**: Activate the invited user
**Recipient**: The person being invited

| # | Timing | Purpose |
|---|--------|---------|
| 1 | Immediate | You've been invited |
| 2 | Day 2 | Reminder if not accepted |
| 3 | Day 5 | Final reminder |

**Copy**: Personalize with inviter's name · explain what they're joining · single CTA to accept · social proof optional

## Retention Emails

### Upgrade to Paid
**Trigger**: Free user shows engagement, or trial ending
**Goal**: Convert free to paid
**Length**: 3–5 emails

**Trigger options**:
- Time-based (trial day 10, 12, 14)
- Behavior-based (hit usage limit, used premium feature)
- Engagement-based (highly active free user)

**Sequence**: Value summary → feature comparison (what they're missing) → social proof → urgency (trial ending) → final last chance

---

### Upgrade to Higher Plan
**Trigger**: User approaching plan limits or accessing higher-tier features
**Goal**: Upsell to next tier
**Format**: Single email or 2–3 email sequence

**Example triggers**: 80% of seat limit · 90% of storage · tried to use higher-tier feature · power user behavior

**Copy**: Acknowledge growth (positive) → show what next tier unlocks → quantify value vs. cost → easy upgrade path

---

### Ask for Review
**Trigger**: Customer milestone (30/60/90 days, key achievement, post-support resolution)
**Goal**: Generate social proof on G2, Capterra, app stores
**Format**: Single email

**Best timing**: After positive support interaction · after measurable result · after renewal
**Avoid**: After billing issues, bugs, or friction moments

**Copy**: Thank them → mention specific milestone → explain why reviews matter → direct link to review platform → short

---

### Proactive Support Offer
**Trigger**: Signs of struggle — usage drop, failed actions, error encounters, stuck in onboarding
**Goal**: Save at-risk user, improve experience
**Format**: Single email

**Example triggers**: Usage dropped week-over-week · multiple failed actions · viewed help docs repeatedly · stuck at same step

**Copy**: Genuine concern tone · specific ("I noticed you...") · offer direct help not just docs link · from support or CSM · no sales pitch

---

### Product Usage Report
**Trigger**: Time-based (weekly, monthly, quarterly)
**Goal**: Demonstrate value, drive engagement, reduce churn
**Format**: Single recurring email

**Include**: Key metrics / activity summary · comparison to previous period · achievements / milestones · suggestions · light CTA
**Examples**: "You saved X hours this month" · "Your team completed X projects" · "You're in the top X% of users"

**Key point**: Make them feel the value. Empty reports are worse than no report.

---

### NPS Survey
**Trigger**: Time-based (quarterly) or post-milestone
**Goal**: Measure satisfaction, identify promoters and detractors
**Format**: Single email

**Best practices**: Just the NPS question first · follow-up form for "why" based on score · personal sender (CEO, founder, CSM) · explain how feedback gets used

**Follow-up by score**:
- Promoters (9–10): Thank + ask for review or referral
- Passives (7–8): Ask what would make it a 10
- Detractors (0–6): Personal outreach to understand issues

---

### Referral Program
**Trigger**: Customer milestone, promoter NPS score, or campaign
**Goal**: Generate referrals
**Format**: Single email or periodic reminders

**Good timing**: After positive NPS · after customer achieves result · after renewal · seasonal campaigns

**Copy**: Remind them of their success → explain referral offer clearly → make sharing easy (unique link) → show what's in it for both sides

## Billing Emails

### Switch to Annual
**Trigger**: Monthly subscriber at renewal time or campaign
**Goal**: Convert monthly to annual (improve LTV, reduce churn)
**Format**: Single email or 2-email sequence

**Value prop**: Calculate exact savings · additional benefits if any · lock in current price · easy one-click switch
**Best timing**: Around monthly renewal · end of year / new year · after 3–6 months of loyalty · price increase announcement

---

### Failed Payment Recovery
**Trigger**: Payment fails
**Goal**: Recover revenue, retain customer
**Length**: 3–4 emails over 7–14 days

| # | Timing | Tone |
|---|--------|------|
| 1 | Day 0 | Friendly — card likely expired |
| 2 | Day 3 | Reminder — service may be interrupted |
| 3 | Day 7 | Urgent — account will be suspended |
| 4 | Day 10–14 | Final — what they'll lose |

**Copy**: Assume accident (card expired, etc.) · clear + direct · no guilt · single CTA to update payment · explain what happens if not resolved

---

### Cancellation Survey
**Trigger**: User cancels subscription
**Goal**: Learn why, opportunity to save
**Format**: Single email (immediate)

**Options**: In-app survey at cancellation (better completion) · follow-up email if skipped · personal outreach for high-value accounts

**Questions**: Primary reason for cancelling · what could we have done better · would anything change your mind · can we help with transition

**Win-back opportunity**: Based on reason, offer targeted save (discount, pause, downgrade, more training)

---

### Upcoming Renewal Reminder
**Trigger**: X days before renewal (14 or 30 days typical)
**Goal**: No surprise charges, opportunity to expand
**Format**: Single email

**Include**: Renewal date + amount · what's included · how to update payment/plan · changes to pricing or features if any · optional upsell

**Required for**: Annual subscriptions, high-value contracts

---

## Usage Emails

### Daily / Weekly / Monthly Summary
**Trigger**: Time-based
**Goal**: Drive engagement, demonstrate value
**Format**: Single recurring email

**Content by frequency**:
- Daily: Notifications, quick stats (high-engagement products only)
- Weekly: Activity summary, highlights, suggestions
- Monthly: Comprehensive report, achievements, ROI if calculable

**Structure**: Key metrics at a glance · achievements · activity breakdown · suggestions / what to try next · CTA to dive deeper

**Personalization required**: Must be relevant to actual usage. Empty or generic summaries are worse than none.

---

### Key Event / Milestone Notifications
**Trigger**: Specific achievement or event
**Goal**: Celebrate, drive continued engagement
**Format**: Single email per event

**Milestone examples**: First [action] completed · 10th/100th [thing] · goal achieved · team collaboration milestone · usage streak

**Copy**: Celebration tone · specific achievement · context (vs. others, vs. before) · what's next / next milestone

## Win-Back Emails

### Expired Trials
**Trigger**: Trial ended without conversion
**Goal**: Convert or re-engage
**Length**: 3–4 emails over 30 days

| # | Timing | Purpose |
|---|--------|---------|
| 1 | Day 1 post-expiry | Trial ended — here's what you're missing |
| 2 | Day 7 | What held you back? (gather feedback) |
| 3 | Day 14 | Incentive offer (discount, extended trial) |
| 4 | Day 30 | Final reach-out, door is open |

**Segment by trial engagement**:
- High engagement → remove friction to convert
- Low engagement → offer fresh start, more onboarding help
- Zero engagement → ask what happened, offer demo or call

---

### Cancelled Customers
**Trigger**: Time after cancellation (30, 60, 90 days)
**Goal**: Win back churned customers
**Length**: 2–3 emails over 90 days

| # | Timing | Purpose |
|---|--------|---------|
| 1 | Day 30 | What's new since you left |
| 2 | Day 60 | We've addressed [common cancel reason] |
| 3 | Day 90 | Special offer to return |

**Copy**: No guilt, no desperation · genuine updates + improvements · personalize on cancel reason if known · make return easy
**Key point**: More likely to return if their reason was addressed

## Campaign Emails

### Monthly Roundup / Newsletter
**Trigger**: Time-based (monthly)
**Goal**: Engagement, brand presence, content distribution
**Format**: Single recurring email

**Content mix**: Product updates + tips · customer stories · educational content · company news · industry insights
**Best practices**: Consistent send day/time · scannable format · one primary CTA · unsubscribes are okay — keeps list healthy

---

### Seasonal Promotions
**Trigger**: Calendar events (Black Friday, New Year, end-of-fiscal, etc.)
**Goal**: Drive conversions with timely offer
**Format**: 2–4 email burst

| Phase | Timing | Purpose |
|-------|--------|---------|
| Announcement | Day 0 | Offer reveal |
| Reminder | Midway | Keep it front of mind |
| Last chance | Final hours | Urgency close |

---

### Product Updates
**Trigger**: New feature release
**Goal**: Adoption, engagement, demonstrate momentum
**Format**: Single email per major release

**Include**: What's new (clear + simple) · why it matters (benefit, not feature) · how to use it (direct link) · who asked for it (community acknowledgment)

**Segment by relevance**: Users who benefit most · users who requested it · power users first (beta feel)

---

### Industry News Roundup
**Trigger**: Time-based (weekly or monthly)
**Goal**: Thought leadership, engagement, brand value
**Format**: Curated newsletter

**Content**: Curated news + links · your take / commentary · what it means for readers · how your product helps
**Best for**: B2B where customers care about industry trends

---

### Pricing Update
**Trigger**: Price change announcement
**Goal**: Transparent communication, minimize churn
**Format**: Single email (or 3-part sequence for major changes)

**Timeline**:
- Announce 30–60 days before change
- Reminder 14 days before
- Final notice 7 days before

**Copy**: Clear + direct + transparent · explain the why (value delivered, costs increased) · grandfather if possible · give options (annual lock-in, downgrade)
