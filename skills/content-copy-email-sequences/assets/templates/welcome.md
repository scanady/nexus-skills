# Welcome Sequence (Post-Signup)

**Trigger**: User signs up (free, trial, or lead magnet download)
**Goal**: Activate, build trust, drive first conversion
**Length**: 5–7 emails over 12–14 days
**Exit conditions**: Converts to paid · completes onboarding action · unsubscribes

---

## Sequence Blueprint

```
Sequence Name:
Trigger: User signs up / downloads lead magnet
Goal: [activation action or trial conversion]

Exit conditions: Converts to paid, completes key action, unsubscribes

```

---

## Email-by-Email Template

**Email 1: Welcome (Immediate)**
- Subject: "Welcome to [Product] — here's your first step"
- Deliver what was promised (lead magnet, access, etc.)
- Single next action — don't overwhelm
- Set expectations for what's coming

**Email 2: Quick Win (Day 1–2)**
- Subject: "Get your first [result] in 10 minutes"
- Enable a small, fast success
- Build confidence and momentum
- Link to helpful resource or tutorial

**Email 3: Story / Why (Day 3–4)**
- Subject: "Why we built [Product]"
- Origin story or mission
- Connect emotionally — show you understand their problem
- No pitch

**Email 4: Social Proof (Day 5–6)**
- Subject: "How [Customer] achieved [Result]"
- Case study or testimonial relatable to their situation
- Soft CTA to explore more

**Email 5: Overcome Objection (Day 7–8)**
- Subject: "I don't have time for X — sound familiar?"
- Address the #1 hesitation your audience has
- Reframe the obstacle and show easy path forward

**Email 6: Core Feature (Day 9–11)**
- Subject: "Have you tried [Feature] yet?"
- Highlight underused but high-value capability
- Specific benefit + direct CTA to try it

**Email 7: Conversion (Day 12–14)**
- Subject: "Ready to [upgrade/buy/commit]?"
- Summarize value delivered in the sequence
- Clear offer with urgency if appropriate
- Risk reversal (guarantee, trial extension)

---

## Segmentation

- **By source**: Lead magnet vs. direct signup → adjust Email 1 delivery
- **By engagement**: Openers who clicked → skip objection email, move to conversion earlier
- **By non-engagement**: No opens by Day 7 → switch to re-engagement fork

---

## Copy Rules

- Email 1–3: zero selling. Build relationship first
- Email 4–5: social proof + objection handling earns the right to ask
- Email 6–7: feature highlight and conversion come after trust is established
- Tone: warm, human, first-person — not broadcast newsletter copy

---

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Email 1 open rate | 50%+ (highest in sequence) |
| Click-through rate | 2–5% |
| Sequence conversion rate | depends on offer |
| Unsubscribe rate | < 0.5% |

---

## Audit Checklist

- [ ] Email 1 sends immediately on trigger
- [ ] Each email has one purpose and one CTA
- [ ] Subject lines 40–60 chars, clear > clever
- [ ] Preview text 90–140 chars, extends subject line
- [ ] Conversion email (Email 7) includes clear offer + risk reversal
- [ ] Exit condition removes converters from sequence
- [ ] Segment rules defined for engaged vs. non-engaged
- [ ] Social proof email uses specific, relatable customer example
