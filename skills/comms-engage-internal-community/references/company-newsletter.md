# Company Newsletter Template

Use for broad internal audiences that need concise, multi-topic updates.

## Required Inputs

- Time window covered
- Major wins and milestones
- People highlights
- Upcoming events or deadlines
- Risks and mitigations (if relevant)

## Structure

1. Header and timeframe
2. Key wins
3. Team highlights
4. Upcoming priorities
5. Closing note

## Writing Rules

- Keep tone informative and grounded.
- Prioritize what changed and why it matters.
- Keep paragraphs short and scannable.
