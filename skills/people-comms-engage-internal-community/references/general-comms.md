# General Internal Comms Template

Fallback template for requests that do not match a specific internal comms format.

## Required Inputs

- Audience
- Objective
- What changed
- Decision or ask
- Timeline

## Structure

1. Context
2. Update
3. Impact
4. Next steps
5. Owner and timeline

## Writing Rules

- Lead with the most important change.
- Make impact and required action explicit.
- End with specific next steps and owners.
