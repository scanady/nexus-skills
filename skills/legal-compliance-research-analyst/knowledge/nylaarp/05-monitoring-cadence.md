# AARP Life Insurance Program — Compliance Monitoring Cadence

**Program:** AARP Life Insurance Program (NYL/AARP)  
**Last Updated:** 2025  
**Purpose:** Defines the recurring monitoring schedule for regulatory changes, enforcement activity, and program-specific compliance triggers. Ensures the standards register, risk watchlist, and language library remain current.

---

## Monitoring Governance

| Role | Responsibility |
|---|---|
| Compliance Manager | Owns the monitoring cadence; escalates material changes to legal and marketing leadership |
| Research Analyst | Executes monitoring tasks; produces intelligence outputs for each cycle |
| Legal Counsel | Reviews Tier 1 regulatory changes; advises on applicability to program materials |
| Marketing Compliance Reviewer | Updates materials review checklists when standards change |
| Product / Underwriting Liaison | Confirms product changes (new states, age band changes, pricing changes) that affect compliance obligations |

## Weekly Monitoring

| Item | Source | Action |
|---|---|---|
| FTC press releases — enforcement actions | ftc.gov/news | Flag any enforcement involving life insurance, senior marketing, telemarketing, or UDAP |
| CFPB blog / newsroom | consumerfinance.gov/about-us/newsroom | Flag any guidance on senior financial protection or direct marketing |
| NYDFS press releases | dfs.ny.gov/reports_and_publications/press_releases | Flag any action involving life insurance advertising or group insurance |
| State AG press releases (CA, FL, TX, NJ, NY) | State AG websites | Flag any action involving senior insurance marketing or UDAP enforcement |
| NAIC committee activity tracker | content.naic.org/committees | Flag any model regulation activity in Life Insurance, Unfair Trade Practices, or Senior Marketing categories |

**Tool:** Google Alerts or equivalent for: "life insurance advertising enforcement," "senior marketing insurance," "FTC insurance," "NAIC model regulation life insurance"

## Monthly Monitoring

| Item | Source | Action |
|---|---|---|
| NAIC complaint index update | NAIC complaint data portal | Review NYL/AARP complaint ratio; flag if ratio increases above 1.0 (expected) |
| FL DFS complaint review | FL DFS insurer complaint data | Review any new complaints related to the AARP Life Insurance Program in FL |
| CA DOI complaint review | CA DOI complaint data | Review any new complaints related to NYL/AARP in CA |
| FTC Telemarketing Sales Rule enforcement | ftc.gov | Monitor for any new enforcement or guidance relevant to call center operations |
| CCPA enforcement update (CA) | CA Privacy Protection Agency | Monitor for any enforcement or guidance affecting email and online advertising |
| TCPA enforcement update | FCC newsroom; TCPA-focused legal alert services | Monitor for any TCPA enforcement relevant to call center or SMS operations |

**Output:** Monthly intelligence memo — one page summarizing any changes requiring action on program materials or operations. Distribute to Compliance Manager and Legal Counsel.

## Quarterly Monitoring

| Item | Source | Action |
|---|---|---|
| **State legislative tracker** — all 50 states | State legislature bill tracking services (NCOIL, MultiState, or equivalent) | Identify any bills in committee or passed affecting life insurance advertising, senior consumer protection, or direct marketing |
| **NAIC model law adoption tracker** | NAIC state action pages for Model #582 (Life Insurance Advertising) and #880 (Unfair Trade Practices) | Check if any state has newly adopted or amended the model regulations |
| **AARP brand guideline review** | Internal AARP brand team or AARP licensing portal | Confirm no updates to AARP co-brand usage rules, anti-ageism standards, or royalty disclosure requirements |
| **NYL product filing review** | NYL Direct product team | Confirm state availability list for all 3 products (especially Guaranteed Acceptance and Living Benefits) is current |
| **AM Best rating confirmation** | AM Best Financial Strength Rating portal | Confirm NYL's A++ rating is current; update language library if rating changes |
| **J.D. Power study** | JDPower.com — Individual Life Insurance Study (annual) | Update rankings references in language library when annual study releases |
| **State delta map review** | This document | Update 04-state-delta-map.md for any state changes identified in quarterly scan |

**Output:** Quarterly compliance intelligence update — includes state legislative summary, product availability confirmation, brand guideline status, and updated standards register if changes occurred.

## Annual Monitoring

| Item | Source | Action |
|---|---|---|
| **Full standards register review** | 01-standards-register.md | Review all controls against current regulatory environment; confirm citations are current; identify any controls that are outdated or require addition |
| **Risk watchlist review** | 02-risk-watchlist.md | Review all 10 risks; update severity ratings if regulatory enforcement pattern has changed; add new risks identified during the year |
| **Compliant language library review** | 03-compliant-language-library.md | Confirm all approved patterns still match current certificate language; update any language where product terms have changed |
| **NAIC national advertising file review** | NAIC advertising file standards | Ensure advertising file format and content meets current NAIC guidance |
| **FTC endorsement guide compliance review** | FTC.gov — Endorsement Guides (16 CFR Part 255) | Confirm AARP royalty disclosure language meets current FTC guidance; note the FTC updated its guides in 2023 |
| **AARP royalty disclosure audit** | AARP licensing agreement; FTC guides | Confirm royalty disclosure language in program materials is accurate and current |
| **Full product availability audit** | NYL Direct product team | Confirm state availability for all products; update 04-state-delta-map.md |
| **Senior marketing landscape review** | CFPB elder financial protection reports; AARP Public Policy Institute reports; NAIC senior bulletin | Identify any new federal or multi-state guidance on senior insurance marketing |

**Output:** Annual compliance intelligence report — comprehensive review of all knowledge base documents; updated standards register; summary of material changes during the year; forward-looking risk radar for next 12 months.

## Trigger-Based Monitoring (Ad Hoc)

These events require an immediate, unscheduled compliance review:

| Trigger | Required Response | Timeline |
|---|---|---|
| FTC, FCC, or CFPB enforcement action against a life insurance direct marketer | Review program materials and operations for the same practices cited in the enforcement action | Within 5 business days |
| Any state AG enforcement involving senior insurance marketing | Assess applicability to program; review materials distributed in that state | Within 5 business days |
| NYDFS examination request or inquiry related to advertising | Pull advertising file; notify legal; full compliance review of materials in question | Immediately |
| NAIC model regulation amendment adopted by any Tier 1 or Tier 2 state (CA, FL, TX, NJ, NY) | Review impact on program materials in that state; update state delta map | Within 10 business days |
| AARP brand guideline update | Review all in-market materials for compliance with updated guidelines; recall or update non-compliant materials | Within 30 days of guideline update |
| NYL product change affecting benefits, pricing, eligibility, or state availability | Update standards register, language library, and state delta map; review all affected in-market materials | Before product change effective date |
| Consumer complaint pattern (3+ complaints with same allegation) | Root cause analysis; review materials for the alleged issue; escalate to legal if regulatory risk exists | Within 10 business days of pattern identification |
| Class action filing or demand letter involving the program | Immediate legal escalation; compliance hold on affected materials pending legal review | Immediately |
| New or changed FTC guidance on endorsements, testimonials, or senior marketing | Full program materials audit against new guidance | Within 30 days |
