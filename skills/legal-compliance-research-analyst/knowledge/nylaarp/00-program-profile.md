## 4. Dual-Brand Compliance Framework

This program operates under two independent compliance standards. Materials must satisfy BOTH before publication.

### Layer 1 — New York Life Insurance Compliance Standards
- NYL internal marketing compliance review process
- NYDFS life insurance advertising rules (NY Insurance Law Article 24; NY Reg 34)
- NAIC Life Insurance Advertising Model Regulation (#582) as adopted by each state
- NAIC Unfair Trade Practices Act (#880) standards
- All-state filing and approval requirements per each state's adopted advertising rules

### Layer 2 — AARP Brand & Licensing Standards
- AARP brand usage guidelines (logo, name, "AARP-branded" descriptor rules)
- AARP anti-ageism advertising commitments (positive aging imagery; no fear/decline framing)
- AARP member trust positioning — materials may not misrepresent AARP's role as a neutral organization
- Class action risk: *Krukas v. AARP* (2018–2020) established that materials claiming AARP-branded coverage is "best for seniors" without adequate disclosure of AARP's royalty interest created litigation exposure (ultimately dismissed, but pattern must be avoided)
