## Quick Reference: Highest-Priority Compliance Rules

1. **Guaranteed Acceptance — 2-year waiting period must be disclosed prominently in all materials.** Never imply full benefit is payable from day one.

2. **Guaranteed Acceptance — Not available in New Jersey.** NJ disclaimer required on all Guaranteed Acceptance materials; NJ residents must be excluded from targeting.

3. **Term Life — Premiums increase every 5-year age band.** Any rate quote or "low rate" claim must include premium escalation disclosure.

4. **AARP is a licensing partner, not an independent endorser.** AARP royalty relationship must be disclosed when materials frame AARP's involvement as a recommendation or endorsement.

5. **Senior audience (50+) — No fear, urgency, or aging-decline framing.** AARP brand standards prohibit ageist marketing. FTC and state laws prohibit exploitative senior marketing tactics.

6. **AARP membership required to purchase.** Must be disclosed in all advertising — not only at application.

7. **Living Benefits — Not available in all states; requires qualifying illness.** Always disclose state availability limitation and qualifying illness requirement.
