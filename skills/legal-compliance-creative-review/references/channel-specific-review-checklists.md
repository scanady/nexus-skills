# Channel-Specific Review Checklists

Use this file for quick execution by channel.

## Paid Social

- Headline/body claims are supportable and bounded.
- Key qualifiers are visible without requiring profile click-through.
- CTA language does not imply guaranteed outcomes.
- Visual claims (badges, seals, charts) have source support.
- Audience targeting assumptions align with suitability expectations.

## Email Campaigns

- Subject and preheader do not overstate outcomes.
- Body copy includes balancing context near core claim.
- Required disclosures are present and readable on mobile.
- Personalization does not imply advice beyond intended scope.
- Links route to pages with consistent, compliant messaging.

## Landing Pages

- Hero claim and primary CTA have adjacent material qualifiers.
- Comparative statements include basis and methodology context.
- Calculators/examples clearly state assumptions.
- Form consent and privacy language are explicit.
- Footnotes map clearly to on-page claims.

## Webinar or Scripted Sales Narrative

- Spoken claims match approved written claims.
- Time-limited statements include basis and conditions.
- Producer role and credentials are represented accurately.
- Objection handling language avoids unsubstantiated absolutes.
- Recording and retention requirements are addressed.

## Print, Brochure, and One-Pagers

- Headlines avoid absolute or universal claims.
- Tables/charts include source attribution and limitations.
- Required legal text is complete and legible.
- Product features and exclusions are balanced.
- Version control and approval metadata are captured.
