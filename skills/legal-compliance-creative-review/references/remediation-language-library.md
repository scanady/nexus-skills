# Remediation Language Library

Use these patterns to preserve marketing intent while reducing compliance risk.

## Core Transformations

| Goal | Risky pattern | Compliant pattern |
|---|---|---|
| Keep strong value proposition | "Guaranteed outcomes" | "Designed to support [goal], subject to product terms and market conditions." |
| Preserve urgency | "Act now or miss out forever" | "Explore this option now to evaluate whether it fits your current needs and timeline." |
| Maintain confidence | "Best solution" | "A strong option for customers with [specific profile], based on [criteria]." |
| Keep comparison angle | "Better than competitors" | "Differs from alternatives in [specific feature], based on [source/method]." |

## Financial and Insurance Copy Blocks

| Use case | Recommended language |
|---|---|
| Performance framing | "Past performance does not guarantee future results. Outcomes vary with market and product factors." |
| Suitability framing | "Appropriateness depends on personal goals, financial situation, and time horizon." |
| Tax framing | "Tax information is general in nature and may not apply to all situations. Consult a qualified tax professional." |
| Guarantee framing | "Guarantees are subject to policy terms and the claims-paying ability of the issuing insurer." |

## Fast Rewrite Workflow

1. Identify the business objective of the original claim.
2. Preserve that objective in plain language.
3. Add the minimum qualifier needed for fair balance.
4. Confirm disclosure proximity and readability.
5. Re-score risk after rewrite.
