# Output Format

```markdown
# Technology Roadmap: [Title — strategic descriptor, not project name]

**Author:** [CIO / CTO]
**Date:** [Current Date]
**Audience:** [Who this narrative is written for]
**Time Horizon:** [e.g., 2026–2028 / 18-month / 3-year]

---

## Strategic Context

[2–3 paragraphs. Why roadmap exists? Business reality, competitive pressure, or strategic ambition driving it? Current state org is moving from? Stakes — what happens if org does not act.]

---

## What This Roadmap Achieves

[1–2 paragraphs. Forward-looking end state summary. When fully realized, what capabilities will org have? How positioned? Gives reader destination before journey.]

---

## [Phase/Horizon 1 Title — Descriptive, e.g., "Building the Foundation"]
**Timeline:** [e.g., Q1–Q3 2026]

[2–3 paragraphs. What's being done and why it comes first. Business capability or outcome delivered. What it sets up for next phase. Key milestones and what "done" looks like.]

---

## [Phase/Horizon 2 Title — Descriptive, e.g., "Scaling and Connecting"]
**Timeline:** [e.g., Q4 2026–Q2 2027]

[2–3 paragraphs. How phase builds on prior one. New capability business gains. Dependency chain. Key milestones.]

---

## [Phase/Horizon 3 Title — Descriptive, e.g., "Accelerating Business Value"]
**Timeline:** [e.g., Q3 2027–Q4 2028]

[2–3 paragraphs. Capstone phase. What this delivers not possible without prior work. How org position fundamentally changed.]

---

## Strategic Tradeoffs

[1–2 paragraphs. What explicitly deprioritized or deferred and why. Frame as disciplined choices — "We chose to delay X to concentrate on Y." Builds credibility.]

---

## Risks and Dependencies

[1–2 paragraphs. Material risks — resourcing, market shifts, tech bets, vendor dependencies. Brief mitigation for each. Focus on risks that would force direction change.]

---

## Investment Summary

[1 paragraph. High-level investment framing — order of magnitude, phasing, or reference to detailed business case.]

---

## The Path Forward

[1–2 paragraphs. Closing. Restate destination. Connect to broader strategic ambitions. If decision or endorsement needed, state directly.]
```

---

## Variants

**Short form ("Roadmap Brief"):**
- Strategic Context (3–4 sentences)
- Phase summaries (1 paragraph each)
- Tradeoffs + Path Forward (1 combined paragraph)

**Internal team version:**
Shift tone to operationally specific and motivational — connect daily execution to strategic purpose, name teams and workstreams, emphasize shared ownership of outcomes.
