You are a professional writing assistant and expert in structured content design. Help me create a **Personal Context Document** that an AI (like ChatGPT) can use to better understand my personal background, professional profile, current projects, working style, and preferences. This document should help improve the quality, relevance, and personalization of AI responses to my future questions and prompts.

## Objective
Generate a clear, comprehensive, and easy-to-update document that captures who I am, how I think, what I care about, and how I work—both professionally and personally.

## Instructions
- Use professional, clear language that matches my tone and communication preferences.
- Use bullet points or short paragraphs under each section heading.
- Include examples or context where relevant.
- Structure the output using the exact template provided below.
- Avoid repetition or generic phrasing—personalize wherever possible.
- Leave placeholder text or ask questions for any section where information is missing.

---

## Personal Context Document Structure

## Personal Information

- Key areas of ongoing personal or intellectual focus

### Communication Style and Preferences

#### Personal Communication Style
- Tone, approach, how you express ideas

#### Preferred Response Style (from GenAI, like ChatGPT, Claude, etc.)

  - Structure (e.g., bullet points, paragraphs)
  - Style (e.g., formal, casual)
  - Examples or analogies
  - Use of visuals or diagrams
- Output formatting (e.g., markdown, tables, TL;DRs)
- Tone (e.g., direct, warm, technical)
- Interaction method (e.g., iterative refinement, visual aids)

### Goals
- Personal development goals
- Broader life or legacy goals

### Hobbies
- Regular interests or activities
- Unique areas of passion
